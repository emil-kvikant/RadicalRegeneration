The Regenerating Revolution Manifesto

We stand for Feminism, Biophilia & confederal communalism.
In other words: Our radical social and ecological Regeneration.
The Radical Regeneration Network nurtures relationships for
Spirit, Theory and Practice guiding
Agitation, Organization and Regeneration.
The network strives to empower everyone to be regenerating.
Regenerating the power to move us from the Capitalocene to the Symbiocene.

With solidarity from the middle of seven generations and from somewhere in the web of life.

Our roots for the Regenerating Revolution:

Radical Change from the root
Regeneration The destruction of competitive, exploitative and degenerative relations and the growth of cooperative, symbiotic and regenerating relations
Network The domination-free, functional creativity enhancing organization of relation-webs
Feminism The destruction of patriarchy and the guidance towards matristic societies
Biophilia The love of nature and the cherishing of indigenous wisdom
Confederal Communalism* The radical democratization of our economic and political spheres
Social Human<->human relationships
Ecological Human<->other-than-human relationships
Relationship A reciprocal connection
Spirit Love, soul, being and psychology
Theory Building intellectual confidence with a new consensus on how to understand and change the world
Practice The tactics, methods and tools for radical regenerating change action
Agitation Inciting of personal and public engagement with the goal of prompting action
Capitalocene In timespan and geographic argument equal to the Anthropocene, but acknowledging that it's not the people demanding the continuous destruction of mother nature but patriarchy, gerontocracy, private property, interest bearing debt and the state as currently expressed in hyper-financialized nation-state capitalism / technofeudalism / hyperimperialism
Symbiocene** An age of symbiopoetic social and ecological regeneration nurtured by mutual aid

* For the more theoretically versed comrades among us: Under confederal communalism we choose to understand a reflexive socialism in the Bourdieuian sense: "[â€¦] reflexivity inclines one to an integrative position which consists in bracketing-off in particular what the opposing theories may owe to the fictious pursuit of difference." Hence under confederal communalism we unite all socialisms that proactively struggle for feminism, ecology, direct democracy, indigenous wisdom and solidarity: I.e. AnarchaFeminism, Ecosocialism, Feministmarxism, Ecomarxism, EcoFeminism, EcoAnarchism, Communalism, libertarian socialism, indigenous and peasant movements, Social Ecology etc.

** The term Symbiocene offers a similar vision to D. Harraway's Chthulucene as they both refer to revelations of new symbiotic science, the science of mutually beneficial relationships. The Symbiocene stands for a time in which the predominant worldview is matricentric, animistic, in which order is aspired not through hierarchy and domination but diversity, emergence, cooperation and symbioses. A time when we experience and make sense of the world through living wholing systems thinking. Apart of many indigenous voices, stories and sciences, like among others Lyla Johnston, Raimon Panikkar and Vandava Shiva, Glenn Albrecht offers an extensive philosophical and psycho-social framework and a new terminology for the Symbiocene age in his book Earth Emotions: New Words for a New World.
